PANDEM-FIT

Never did we worry we were not fit
until it suddenly became a legal obligation to sit.
With limited tonic to just sit and drink
our bored as fuck minds started to think!
So here we are working out at home, taking a stand,
occasionally with headbands on and wine in hand!
